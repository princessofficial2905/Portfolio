About me

A cross-functional technical foundation, shaped into calm, usable systems.

I am a Mechanical and Automation Engineering graduate from IGDTUW who blends technology, testing, documentation, automation, AI tools, and storytelling to build practical digital systems. My strongest pattern is noticing messy workflows, breaking them down, and turning them into something clearer: a test report, a CRM process, a content system, a documentation kit, or a portfolio-ready project.

I work comfortably between structured QA thinking and creative execution. That means I can inspect a broken user flow, clean a CRM dataset, write careful documentation, build a static website, prepare AI training-data style image references, and still care about tone, layout, and how the final thing feels to a human.
